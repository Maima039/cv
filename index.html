Summary

Responsible, motivated researcher in nanotechnology and electrochemistry.

Reseach Experiences

  • Eutectic salt mixture (NaCl/ZnCl2) etching by furnace to synthesize porous MXene and applied it as anode material for Lithium-ion/Dual-ion battery.
  • Carboxyl anhydride functionalized graphite for Dual-ion battery cathode.
  • Tunable growth of MnCo2O4.5 via graphene quantum dots in hydrothermal reaction for high performance supercapacitor electrode.
